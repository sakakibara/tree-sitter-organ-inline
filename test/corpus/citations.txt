==================
Simple citation
==================
[cite:@key]
---

(inline_content
  (citation
    (citation_reference
      (citation_key))))

==================
Citation with style
==================
[cite/style:@key]
---

(inline_content
  (citation
    (citation_style)
    (citation_reference
      (citation_key))))

==================
Citation with prefix and suffix
==================
[cite:prefix @key suffix]
---

(inline_content
  (citation
    (citation_reference
      (citation_prefix)
      (citation_key)
      (citation_suffix))))

==================
Citation with multiple references
==================
[cite:@key1; @key2]
---

(inline_content
  (citation
    (citation_reference
      (citation_key))
    (citation_reference
      (citation_key))))
