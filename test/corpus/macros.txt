==================
Simple macro
==================
{{{name}}}
---

(inline_content
  (macro
    (macro_name)))

==================
Macro with arguments
==================
{{{name(arg1, arg2)}}}
---

(inline_content
  (macro
    (macro_name)
    (macro_argument)
    (macro_argument)))

==================
Macro mixed with text
==================
before {{{macro}}} after
---

(inline_content
  (plain_text)
  (macro
    (macro_name))
  (plain_text))
